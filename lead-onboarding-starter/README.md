# Lead → Onboarding (Starter)

**What it does**  
- When a Google Form is submitted the zap: creates a Trello card, sends a welcome email, updates the same Google Sheets Row


**Built in Zapier**  
1) Trigger: Google Sheets – New Spreadsheet Row  
2) Trello - Create Card "Row_Name - Onboarding"
3) Gmail - Send Email to Row_Email
4) Google Sheets - Update Row (Status & Card URL)

**Set up outside Zapier**  
- Google Form linked to a Sheet  
- Add two columns: Status, TrelloCardURL
- Trello Workspace / List

**Test**  
Submit the form once and verify email, card, and sheet updates.
